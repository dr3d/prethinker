# Boundary Probe: Negative and Exception Answer Surface Ladder

## Passage

The routing office schedules delivery windows for partner reports, but it does
not control the content of those reports. The office also has no editorial veto
over the reports after they are submitted.

The emergency review protocol covers intake reviews and release notes. Archived
notices are outside the protocol, so archived notices are not reviewed under
that emergency rule.

Every staff badge must be renewed weekly except visitor badges. Visitor badges
are exempt from weekly renewal and expire after one day instead.

The matching service automatically assigns routine cases to the standard queue.
Cases marked as sensitive are not assigned automatically; a supervisor must
route each sensitive case by manual override.

The finance desk may draft the budget memo and the audit desk may review it.
Only the oversight chair may approve the budget memo. The review team is not
allowed to approve the budget memo.
