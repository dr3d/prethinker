## Passage

Cedar Trial 18 was a civic review session to select the archive custodian for
the 2024 archive cycle. The Northern Slate delegate Mira Voss defeated the
Southern Slate delegate Taro Ennis 7-4 to earn her third custodian appointment.
The session was held on April 9, 2025, at Lumen Court in West Arlen, Briar
County. Because this was the eighteenth Cedar Trial, the filing office
temporarily suspended the ordinary decimal label and used the archival label
"Cedar Trial XVIII" on the cover sheet. In this passage, CTA stands for Cedar
Trial Assembly, not Central Transit Authority. After the vote, Mira Voss was
named archive custodian, and Taro Ennis was named alternate custodian.
