# Remaining-Share Arithmetic Ladder Source

The river split memo says the main chute carries two thirds of the runoff. The
memo says the remaining runoff enters the reserve channel. A separate service
ditch carries only drain water and is not part of the runoff split.

The seat allocation note divides the hearing room into two public blocks. The
east block receives three fifths of the seats, and the west block receives two
fifths of the seats.

The credit ledger lists a total of 90 review credits. The north account receives
two thirds of the review credits, and the south account receives the rest.

The sample plan begins with 48 sealed samples. Thirty samples go to archive
testing, and the remainder goes to field validation. The damaged-sample count
is tracked in a separate quality table.

The grant schedule assigns 120 units across two named pools. The primary pool
gets 75 units, and the residual pool receives every unassigned unit from that
same 120-unit schedule.

The warehouse note divides one shipment into red, blue, and green shelves. Red
receives one half of the shipment, blue receives one quarter, and green receives
the remaining one quarter.
