## Assembly Summary

Riverside Trial Assembly 14 was held on March 6, 2024 at Rowan Hall in the city
of Port Cedar. The meeting belonged to the 2023 planning cycle. The event label
used in the filing system was Trial Assembly XIV, because the organizers used
Roman numerals on the archival cover sheet.

## Participant Note

The North Panel represented the blue docket at Riverside Trial Assembly 14.
The East Panel represented the green docket. The chair recorded that the North
Panel prevailed after the final review round.

## Venue Note

Rowan Hall was the named venue for the assembly. Port Cedar was the city where
the venue stood, and Gray Harbor County was the county listed on the building
permit.

## Acronym Note

The bulletin abbreviates the Riverside Trial Assembly as RTA. In this packet,
RTA stands for Riverside Trial Assembly, not Regional Transit Annex.

## Outcome Note

The assembly determined the lead reviewer for the 2023 planning cycle. After
the vote, Mira Solen was named lead reviewer. The alternate reviewer was Dax
Irwin.
