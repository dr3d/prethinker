# Boundary Probe: Inline Alias and Alternate Label Ladder

## Passage

The Harbor Review League (HRL) convened River Session 9 to approve the dock
repair calendar. The Maritime Audit Council (MAC) observed the session but did
not vote. In this passage, DRC stands for Dock Repair Calendar, not District
Records Center. The filing office usually labels River Session records with
Greek letters; under that ordinary convention, River Session 9 would have been
filed as River Session Iota. Because the archive switched to decimal labels for
that year, the cover sheet instead used River Session 9.
