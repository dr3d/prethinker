# Purpose/Use Surface Ladder Source

Maintenance technicians use the ultrasonic wand to locate hidden weld cracks.
The wand measures echo delay and wall thickness during the same inspection. The
wand is not used for final certification.

Reservoir analysts use the blue dye tracer to map water movement through the
test tank. The tracer records current direction and flow speed after release.
The tracer is not a sediment sampler.

Archivists use the multispectral scanner to recover faded annotations on old
forms. The scanner captures infrared bands and pigment contrast. It is not used
to authenticate signatures.

Warehouse clerks use the bench scale to verify kit completeness before sealing
shipment cartons. The scale measures total mass and flags weight differences.
The scale does not identify the missing part.

Flight instructors use the training simulator to practice engine-out approaches.
The simulator records throttle position and descent angle. The simulator is not
the final licensing test.
