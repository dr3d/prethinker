# Target Anchor Density Ladder

The routing analysts, who had reviewed the outage log, joined not the
contingency desk but the escalation desk after the morning outage review and
before the vendor callback. The outage review was the anchor for the move; the
escalation desk was the team they joined.

The audit liaison was appointed to the variance board, not to the records-freeze
task force, following the records freeze and ahead of the policy recap. The
freeze was the prior event; the variance board was the body that received the
appointment.

The calibration crew attached the spare sensor kit to the lab rack before the
sensor retest, but the crew itself was attached to the night-shift repair unit
after the retest failed. The repair unit was the assignment target for the crew.

The intake assistants entered the orientation block first and were then enrolled
in the evidence-handling course, not the attendance desk roster. The orientation
block supplied the timing context; the evidence-handling course was the program
they entered.

After the archive migration and before fee reconciliation, the harbor reviewers
moved from the archive queue onto the permit queue. The migration was the anchor
event, and the permit queue was the work queue they moved onto.
