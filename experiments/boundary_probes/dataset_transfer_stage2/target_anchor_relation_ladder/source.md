# Target Anchor Relation Ladder

The routing analysts joined the contingency desk after the morning outage
review. The review supplied the timing reason for the transfer, but the desk
was the team they joined.

The audit liaison was appointed to the variance board following the records
freeze. The freeze was the prior event; the variance board was the body that
received the appointment.

The calibration crew was attached to the night-shift repair unit before the
sensor retest. The retest was scheduled later, while the repair unit was the
assignment target.

The intake assistants were enrolled in the evidence-handling course during the
spring orientation block. The orientation block gave the time window, but the
course was the program entered.

The harbor reviewers moved onto the permit queue after the archive migration.
The migration was the anchor event, while the permit queue was the work queue
they moved onto.
