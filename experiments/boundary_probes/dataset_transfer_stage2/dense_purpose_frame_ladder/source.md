# Dense Purpose Frame Ladder Source

Inspection engineers diagnose cable faults in the depot using two laboratory
methods: thermal imaging and pulse reflectometry. In thermal imaging, the camera
shows heat signatures along the insulation. In pulse reflectometry, the meter
measures reflection time and signal loss along the cable. Neither method is used
for customer billing.

Archive conservators restore water-damaged manuscripts with two bench methods:
a solvent bath and fiber microscopy. The solvent bath loosens salt deposits from
the paper. Fiber microscopy checks swelling and strand separation after the
bath. Neither method is used to appraise market value.

Soil analysts compare field samples using chromatography and isotope counting.
Chromatography separates the volatile compounds. Isotope counting measures
carbon and nitrogen ratios for each sample. Neither method is used to choose
the planting schedule.

Athletic coaches evaluate sprint technique using video timing and a force
plate. Video timing records split intervals. The force plate records pressure
patterns and launch asymmetry during takeoff. Neither method is the official
race result.
