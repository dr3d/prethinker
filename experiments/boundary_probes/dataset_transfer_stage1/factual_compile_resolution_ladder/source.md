## Packet Header

Packet ID: BP-DT-Factual-001.
Source title: North Quay Field Bulletin.
Publication form: field bulletin.

This bulletin summarizes ordinary observations from a harbor training week. It
contains direct factual statements only. It does not ask the reader to infer a
theme, choose among answer options, or interpret a narrator's attitude.

## Office Note

The records team worked from a small office beside the quay. The office had one
desk, a cabinet, and a narrow window facing the loading gate.

## Demonstration Note

At the training fair, finalists demonstrated their work by presenting their
prototypes to the review board. They did not demonstrate their work by taking
an examination or by submitting a written packet alone.

## Marker Movement Note

Drift marker NQ-7 traveled with the tide on Tuesday morning. During that trip,
the marker paused for several hours while a maintenance crew moored it to
replace a lamp. After the lamp was replaced, the marker continued downriver.

## Spill Note

After the dye bottle spilled across the intake table, the volunteers helped
clean the table and dry the forms. The supervisor recorded the spill but did
not do the cleanup herself.

## Runoff Log

The runoff log listed grit, solvent, and medical waste as the contaminants
found in the east drain sample. The log did not list swimmers, fish, or city
names as contaminants.

## Monday Roster Note

On Monday, only the father was at home. The mother worked at the clinic that
day, the daughter attended class, and the son was at a workshop.

## Trainee Arrival Note

Ten trainees arrived late after the ferry delay. Twelve trainees came by
bicycle on time, and four trainees walked from the hostel.
