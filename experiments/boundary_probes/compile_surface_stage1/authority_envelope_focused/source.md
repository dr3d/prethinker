# Boundary Probe: Authority Envelope Focused Miss

## Packet Header

Packet ID: BP-AUTH-001.
Subject file: sidewalk canopy permit SC-17.
Packet date: May 9, 2026.

The packet concerns whether sidewalk canopy permit SC-17 remained operative
while an amendment was pending.

## Staff Routing Note

Permit coordinator Lena Vale prepared a staff routing note on May 3, 2026. The
note forwarded the applicant's amendment request to legal review. The routing
note did not decide the permit's legal status and did not approve or deny the
amendment.

## Legal Memo

City Solicitor Adrian Bell, Esq. signed legal memo LM-4 on May 4, 2026. The
memo states: "The original permit remains operative until an amendment is
formally issued. A filed amendment request alone does not suspend the original
permit."

## Warden Action

Tree Warden Mira Holt issued amendment SC-17-A1 on May 6, 2026. The amendment
changed the canopy support spacing requirement from 8 feet to 6 feet and became
effective immediately upon issuance.

## Applicant Letter

Applicant North Pier Markets LLC sent a letter on May 7, 2026 saying it
believed the amendment had been approved earlier. The letter was received for
the file, but it was not a legal determination.
