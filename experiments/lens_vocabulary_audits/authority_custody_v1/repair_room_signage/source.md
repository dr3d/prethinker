# Repair Room Signage Packet

The neighborhood repair room opened packet RRS-8 after a tool-cabinet sign conflicted with the official log.

Official tool register TR-8 was filed by coordinator Bea Cruz on 2026-11-01. TR-8 names bench captain Omar Vale as custody holder for the soldering cabinet key and marks the cabinet as controlled equipment.

Draft recommendation DR-8 was prepared by outreach helper Mina Cho on 2026-11-02. DR-8 proposed lending the soldering cabinet key to any member who completed the short safety quiz, but it remained draft pending workshop board review.

Governing tool rule GT-8 says controlled equipment keys stay with the bench captain unless the workshop board approves a checkout class.

On 2026-11-03, the workshop board voted to allow checkout only to certified mentors during supervised repair hours.

Controlling finding CF-8 was signed by board chair Ren Ito on 2026-11-04. CF-8 says the board vote controls checkout scope and Omar Vale remains custody holder.

A copied hallway sign HS-8 says "quiz-complete members may borrow the soldering cabinet key." HS-8 is noncontrolling because it copied DR-8 before board review and omitted the board vote and CF-8.

Access to the soldering cabinet key is controlled by bench captain Omar Vale.
