# Theater Prop Cage Packet

The youth theater opened packet TPC-3 after a rehearsal guide disagreed with the prop cage register.

Official prop register PR-3 was filed by stage manager Eli Noor on 2026-12-01. PR-3 names prop master Kira Moss as custody holder for the prop cage key and labels the cage as restricted storage.

Staff note SN-3 was written by rehearsal lead Petra Shaw on 2026-12-02. SN-3 recommended letting assistant directors fetch lightweight props during rehearsal.

Draft recommendation DR-3 was prepared by logistics volunteer Theo Ames on 2026-12-03. DR-3 proposed giving every cast captain a weekend prop cage key, but it remained draft pending safety committee review.

Safety storage rule SS-3 governs prop cage access. SS-3 says the prop cage key stays with the prop master unless the safety committee authorizes a named checkout group.

On 2026-12-04, the safety committee approved assistant-director access during supervised rehearsal only and rejected weekend cast-captain checkout.

Controlling finding CF-3 was entered by the theater board on 2026-12-05. CF-3 says the safety committee decision controls prop cage access and Kira Moss remains custody holder.

A copied rehearsal guide RG-3 says "cast captains may keep weekend prop keys." RG-3 is noncontrolling because it copied DR-3 and ignored the safety committee rejection and CF-3.

Access to the prop cage key is controlled by prop master Kira Moss.
