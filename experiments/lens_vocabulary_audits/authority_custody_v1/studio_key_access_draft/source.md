# Studio Key Access Draft

The ceramics studio opened packet SKA-5 after a draft key-access memo was posted early.

Official key register OK-5 was filed by studio clerk Talia Wynn on 2027-03-05. OK-5 names kiln supervisor Marco Bell as custody holder for the glaze room key and marks the room as restricted material storage.

Draft recommendation DR-5 was prepared by program assistant Hadi Noor on 2027-03-06. DR-5 proposed weekend glaze room checkout for all advanced students, but it remained draft pending facilities legal review.

Staff note SN-5 was written by teaching artist Ren Fox on 2027-03-07. SN-5 recommended weekend checkout for two monitor-trained students only.

Material storage rule MS-5 says restricted material keys stay with the kiln supervisor unless facilities approves a delegate list.

Facilities decision FD-5 was issued on 2027-03-08. FD-5 allowed weekend checkout only to two monitor-trained students and rejected all-student checkout.

Controlling finding CF-5 says FD-5 controls weekend access and Marco Bell remains custody holder.

A copied studio memo SM-5 says "all advanced students may check out the glaze room key on weekends." SM-5 is noncontrolling because it copied DR-5 before legal review and omitted FD-5 and CF-5.

Access to the glaze room key is controlled by kiln supervisor Marco Bell.
