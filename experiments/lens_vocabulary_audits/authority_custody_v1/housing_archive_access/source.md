# Housing Archive Access Packet

The Larkspur Housing Co-op reviewed access to tenant archive carton A-204 after a former officer asked to remove inspection photographs.

The official archive register AR-77 was filed on 2026-03-12 by keeper Sol Chen. AR-77 lists carton A-204, identifies the photographs as tenant-record material, and names archive steward Mina Rao as custody holder.

Property manager Leif Moran wrote staff note SN-4 on 2026-03-13. The note suggested allowing the former officer to view the photographs in the office, but it did not authorize removal.

Draft recommendation DR-11 was prepared by the records subcommittee on 2026-03-14. DR-11 proposed scanning the photographs and returning the originals to the former officer. It stayed draft pending a board vote.

The co-op records bylaw B-2 governs tenant-record cartons. B-2 says originals remain in archive custody unless a board vote or court order changes custody.

On 2026-03-15, the co-op board voted 6-1 to keep the originals in carton A-204 and allow only supervised inspection copies.

Municipal records order MO-9 was issued by the city records officer on 2026-03-16. It confirmed that tenant photographs could be viewed by the former officer but not removed from archive custody.

District court order DC-20 was issued on 2026-03-18. DC-20 controls any later removal request and requires a separate written release naming each photograph.

The appeals panel issued controlling finding AF-5 on 2026-03-19. AF-5 states that Mina Rao remains custody holder, that the board vote controls supervised copies, and that DC-20 controls removal requests.

A copied newsletter item NL-8 says "the former officer may take the photos after scanning." NL-8 is noncontrolling because it copied draft recommendation DR-11 and omitted the later board vote.

Access to carton A-204 is controlled by archive steward Mina Rao during office hours.
