# Library Cart Policy Draft

The library opened packet LCP-2 after mobile-cart checkout rules were revised.

Official equipment register LR-2 was filed by access clerk Rowan Hale on 2027-01-10. LR-2 names circulation lead Dev Singh as custody holder for the outreach laptop cart and marks the cart as staff-controlled equipment.

Draft recommendation DR-2 was prepared by outreach aide Lena Park on 2027-01-11. DR-2 proposed allowing trained student interns to check out the outreach laptop cart for school visits, but it remained draft pending library director approval.

Staff note SN-2 was written by branch manager Jules Chen on 2027-01-12. SN-2 recommended a narrower pilot for two senior interns only.

Equipment rule ER-2 governs staff-controlled carts. ER-2 says checkout stays with circulation staff unless the library director approves a named delegate class.

Director order DO-2 was issued on 2027-01-13. DO-2 allowed checkout by two named senior interns for school visits, but it did not approve all trained interns.

Controlling finding CF-2 says DO-2 controls the pilot and Dev Singh remains custody holder.

Access to the outreach laptop cart is controlled by circulation lead Dev Singh.
