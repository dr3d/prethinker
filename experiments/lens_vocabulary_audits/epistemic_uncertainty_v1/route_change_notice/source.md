# Epistemic Uncertainty Probe: Route Change Notice

The neighborhood shuttle board issued Route Notice RN-22 on 2026-05-03.

The notice verified that stop Pine Square was closed for sidewalk repair. The
detour map is provisional until the curb survey is finished.

An earlier driver report said the eastbound loop skipped Harbor Court, but that
report is disputed by the dispatch radio log. The board withdrew the first
passenger-count table after finding duplicate clipboard entries.

The notice states that weekend service is pending a vote by the operations
committee. It says the final pickup order is not stated in the notice.

The rain-delay warning was superseded by a later operations bulletin. A claim
that the north shelter was inaccessible is unsupported because the inspection
photo shows the ramp clear. From the stop list and the closed-stop notice, the
planner inferred that Pine Square riders should board at Oak Terrace.

The notice rules out fare increases for the detour period.
