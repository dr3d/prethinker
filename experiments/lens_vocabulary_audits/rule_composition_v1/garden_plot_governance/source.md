# Rule Composition Probe: Garden Plot Governance

The Eastbank Garden Committee published its 2026 plot-transfer procedure.

The base rule says a plot holder may transfer a plot only to a person already
on the active waitlist. The eligibility condition for receiving a transfer is
having attended at least three workdays in the current season.

The threshold for a hardship transfer is two missed watering shifts in a
calendar month. A hardship exception allows the transfer even when the
recipient has attended only one workday, but only if the committee records a
medical absence note.

Plot transfers normally require a simple majority vote of the committee. A
transfer that bypasses the workday condition requires a two-thirds vote and at
least five committee members present.

If the hardship exception conflicts with the anti-speculation rule, the
anti-speculation rule overrides the hardship exception. The anti-speculation
rule has precedence over every transfer exception.

If the monthly meeting lacks quorum, the fallback rule is to defer the transfer
vote to the next regular meeting.
