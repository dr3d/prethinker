# Evidence Provenance Probe: Garden Water Log

The North Lot Garden board reviewed Water Log WL-3.

Board member Theo Park commissioned a soil moisture test from Field Helper
Samira Das after the east beds wilted. Samira prepared the test card and dated
it 2026-05-01.

Theo submitted the card during the Saturday board meeting. The board admitted
the card into the water log and relied on it when assigning two extra watering
turns to the east beds.

The log says the original card was later amended because one bed label was
typed as E-7 instead of E-1. The amended card is kept in the lockbox under the
seed table.
