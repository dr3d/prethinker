# Transition Delta Probe: Policy Threshold Revision

Revision Notice RN-31 was issued on 2027-01-06.

Memo R-19 supersedes Bulletin C-14 for parcel intake. Before Memo R-19,
standard parcels were accepted only up to 18 kg. Memo R-19 raises the standard
parcel weight limit to 22 kg.

Bulletin C-14 required a supervisor note for cold-pack parcels. Memo R-19
removes that supervisor-note requirement.

Memo R-19 adds a photo check for parcels with oversized labels.

The approving body remains Operations Review, and the intake desk remains
Counter 2.
