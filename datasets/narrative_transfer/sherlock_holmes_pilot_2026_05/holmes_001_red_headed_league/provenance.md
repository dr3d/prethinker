## Extraction Method

The fixture was generated from the Gutenberg HTML page by locating the `h2` heading for "II. THE RED-HEADED LEAGUE" and collecting paragraph content until the `h2` heading for "III. A CASE OF IDENTITY". HTML wrappers, navigation, collection front matter, other stories, and the Gutenberg license block were stripped from the source files.

`source_original.txt` preserves the extracted story text as plain text. `source.md` is a faithful Markdown rendering of the same extracted section, preserving paragraph breaks, dialogue punctuation, em-dashes, smart quotes, and italic spans where represented in the HTML.

## Preserved

- Dialogue and paragraph order
- Victorian punctuation, smart quotes, em-dashes, and spelling from the Gutenberg text
- The story heading
- Internal narrative facts, aliases, rules, and explanations exactly as represented in the story

## Stripped

- Gutenberg navigation and collection table of contents
- License header and footer from the source text files
- Other adventures in the collection
- HTML layout wrappers
