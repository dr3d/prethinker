### §2. Original Lease (ROW-LSE-001 / LSE-2024-0331-007)

The original lease was executed on March 31, 2024 between Bridgemarrow Properties LP (landlord) and Quillvale Tea & Provisions Co. (tenant) for the premises located at Unit 4B, 1718 Catalpa Trace.

Original term: five (5) years, commencing April 1, 2024 and ending March 30, 2029.
Original base rent: $4,250 per month.
Use clause: retail tea and dry-goods sales.

The original lease is currently ACTIVE as amended by AMD-2026-014 (see §8 below).

### §3. Proposed Amendment Draft 1 (ROW-AMD-001 / PA-2026-014-D1)

Dated February 9, 2026. Authored by Bridgemarrow's transaction counsel. The draft proposed the following modifications to LSE-2024-0331-007:

- Term extension: two (2) additional years, with a new termination date of March 30, 2031.
- Rent escalation: base rent to $4,650 per month, effective April 1, 2026.
- Right of First Refusal: ROFR granted to Quillvale on **Unit 4C** (an adjacent unit on the same floor), with no specified exercise window in the draft.

This document is a **draft** and was not executed in this form. See §6 (revised draft), §7 (executed amendment), and §11 (voided document notice).

### §5. Tenant Letter of Intent (ROW-LOI-001 / TLOI-2026-019)

Dated February 17, 2026. Authored and signed by Brindly Achterheim, Managing Partner, Quillvale Tea & Provisions Co.

Letter expresses Quillvale's intent to enter into a lease amendment in substantially the form of PA-2026-014-D1. The body of the letter contains a "non-binding" footer in the following terms:

> "This letter is a statement of intent only and is expressly non-binding upon either party. No legal obligation, encumbrance, right of first refusal, rent escalation, or extension of term arises from this letter, and the parties retain full discretion to negotiate, amend, or decline to proceed. Neither this letter nor any reliance upon it shall be construed as forming a contract or as a waiver of any right under the original lease LSE-2024-0331-007."

The Quillvale letter of intent is therefore source-attributed expression of tenant intent. It is **non-binding** as to either party. The Register accepts the letter for the administrative record but assigns no legal effect to its content.

### §6. Counsel Opinion (ROW-OP-001 / COP-2026-031)

Dated March 4, 2026. Authored by Mortlock Estavanico, Esq., outside counsel to Bridgemarrow Properties LP.

The opinion addresses the proposed amendment PA-2026-014-D1 in light of the tenant letter of intent TLOI-2026-019. Counsel's conclusions are:

1. **PA-2026-014-D1 is enforceable subject to execution.** The draft language is sufficient to create an amendment if executed by both parties, but until execution the draft has no operative effect.
2. **Right of First Refusal mechanics.** Counsel cautions that the ROFR as drafted is silent on exercise window and on the form of landlord's notice. Counsel recommends a 30-day exercise window and notice by registered mail.
3. **Tenant letter of intent.** Counsel confirms that TLOI-2026-019 is non-binding on its face and does not bind the landlord; the landlord retains full discretion to negotiate further, withdraw, or modify the amendment terms prior to execution.
4. **Recommendations to landlord.** Counsel recommends modifications to the draft: include an execution deadline beyond which the proposed amendment expires; clarify rent commencement language to address the case in which execution post-dates April 1, 2026; clarify ROFR exercise procedure.

This counsel opinion is the source-attributed legal opinion of Bridgemarrow's counsel. It is not a binding judicial ruling and is offered to the parties as professional advice. It is logged in the Register under ROW-OP-001 for administrative completeness only.

### §7. Proposed Amendment Draft 2 (ROW-AMD-002 / PA-2026-014-D2)

Dated March 18, 2026. Incorporates counsel's recommendations from COP-2026-031. Changes from PA-2026-014-D1:

- Execution deadline added: **April 30, 2026**. If not executed by both parties by that date, the proposed amendment lapses.
- Rent commencement clarified: rent escalation to $4,650 per month becomes effective on the later of (a) April 1, 2026, or (b) the execution date of the amendment.
- ROFR clarified: 30-day exercise window after landlord's notice to tenant by registered mail.
- ROFR unit unchanged in this draft: **Unit 4C**.

This draft was the basis for execution but is itself superseded by the executed instrument AMD-2026-014.

### §8. Executed Amendment (ROW-AMD-003 / AMD-2026-014)

Executed on **April 7, 2026** by both Bridgemarrow Properties LP (signator: Reedwell Phaneuf, General Partner) and Quillvale Tea & Provisions Co. (signator: Brindly Achterheim, Managing Partner).

The executed instrument incorporates the substance of PA-2026-014-D2 with the following final-execution changes (which were negotiated in the last week before signing and documented at AMD-2026-014, page 7):

- ROFR unit is **Unit 4D**, not Unit 4C. The parties agreed during the closing week to substitute Unit 4D (which is contiguous to the rear of Unit 4B and was identified during a site walkthrough as the operationally more useful adjacency for Quillvale's planned dry-goods expansion). Unit 4C, which had been the subject of the earlier drafts, became unavailable when its existing tenant exercised a holdover right.
- All other terms as set forth in PA-2026-014-D2.

Rent commencement: under the rule "later of April 1, 2026 or execution date," rent escalates to $4,650 per month effective **April 7, 2026**.

Term: new termination date March 30, 2031.

The executed amendment is the operative governing instrument as to the matters it addresses. The amended lease is referred to in the Register as "LSE-2024-0331-007 as amended by AMD-2026-014."

### §10. Register Correction (ROW-CORR-001 / CORR-2026-008)

Dated April 14, 2026. Issued by Register Clerk Honora Pelhambridge.

Subject: Correction of REG-E-2026-061 with respect to the Right of First Refusal unit.

Finding: The executed amendment AMD-2026-014, at page 7, identifies the ROFR unit as **Unit 4D**, not Unit 4C as recorded in the initial register entry REG-E-2026-061. The initial entry is hereby corrected. The amended lease grants Quillvale Tea & Provisions Co. a Right of First Refusal on **Unit 4D**, subject to the 30-day exercise window and registered-mail notice procedure stated in AMD-2026-014.

No other terms of REG-E-2026-061 are changed by this correction. The amended lease term-end (March 30, 2031), rent effective date (April 7, 2026), and monthly rent ($4,650) all remain as recorded.

A corrected register entry has been generated: see §11 (REG-E-2026-061-R).
