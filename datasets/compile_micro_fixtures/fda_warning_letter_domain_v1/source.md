# FDA Warning Letter Domain Micro Fixture V1

U.S. Food and Drug Administration
Office of Pharmaceutical Quality Operations

May 14, 2025

WARNING LETTER

To: Marigold Sterile Products, Inc.
Facility: Marigold Sterile Products, Inc., 12 Harbor Road, Camden, New Jersey 08102
FEI: 3012345678
This micro excerpt does not show an individual signatory.

FDA inspected the Camden facility from February 3, 2025 through February 7, 2025.
FDA reviewed the firm's February 21, 2025 response to the Form FDA 483.
FDA also considered a prior warning letter issued to the firm on August 12, 2022.
FDA held a regulatory teleconference with the firm on April 3, 2025.

1. Your quality unit failed to review batch production records before release for lots A-104 and A-105. This is a violation of 21 CFR 211.192.

2. Written procedures designed to prevent microbiological contamination of drug products purporting to be sterile were not established and followed. This is a violation of 21 CFR 211.113(b).

These violations cause your drug products to be adulterated within the meaning of section 501(a)(2)(B) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.

Within 15 working days of receipt of this letter, send FDA a written response that identifies corrective actions and supporting documentation. Failure to adequately address this matter may result in withholding approval of pending applications or export certificates.

We strongly recommend that you engage a consultant qualified as set forth in 21 CFR 211.34.

This letter is not intended to be an all-inclusive list of violations at your facility. You are responsible for investigating and determining the causes of any violations and preventing their recurrence.
