# NTSB Investigation Domain Micro Fixture V1

National Transportation Safety Board

## Aviation Investigation Preliminary Report

Accident Number: DCA26MA024
Report status: preliminary
Report issued: November 7, 2025

On November 5, 2025, about 1713:30 eastern standard time, UPS flight 2976, a Boeing MD-11F registered as N259UP, was involved in an accident near Louisville, Kentucky. The flight was operated by UPS Airlines. The report states that no final probable cause or contributing factor has been determined in this preliminary report.

Casualty Summary:

| Scope | Fatal | Serious | Minor |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Crew | 0 | 0 | 0 |
| Passenger | 0 | 0 | 0 |

Weather at the time of the accident included 10 miles visibility and wind from 310 degrees at 4 knots.

The cockpit voice recorder was not recovered at the scene. The flight data recorder was recovered and sent to the NTSB recorder laboratory.

On November 14, 2025, the FAA issued Emergency AD 2025-23-53, superseding Emergency AD 2025-23-51 and expanding the affected-airplane scope to include MD-11F airplanes.
