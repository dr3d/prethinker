# FDA Warning Letter Environmental-Monitoring Detail Micro Fixture V1

U.S. Food and Drug Administration
Office of Pharmaceutical Quality Operations

June 9, 2025

WARNING LETTER

To: Northbank Sterile Solutions LLC
Facility: Northbank Sterile Solutions LLC, 44 Creek Road, Newark, Delaware 19702
FEI: 3022334455

FDA inspected the Newark facility from March 10, 2025 through March 14, 2025.
FDA reviewed the firm's March 28, 2025 response to the Form FDA 483.

1. Your firm failed to thoroughly investigate environmental monitoring excursion results and out-of-specification endotoxin results. This is a violation of 21 CFR 211.192.

Environmental monitoring excursions: Since 2023, repeated viable-air and surface-monitoring results exceeded your firm's action limits in ISO 5 and ISO 7 areas. Several investigations closed with "no assignable root cause" even when the same organisms recurred in the same room.

OOS endotoxin results: Your firm invalidated multiple OOS endotoxin results without a scientifically justified laboratory error. The investigations did not evaluate whether terminal sterilization cycle execution contributed to the failures.

Your response is inadequate because it does not explain how the investigation procedure will ensure scientifically justified root-cause determinations.

2. Your firm failed to maintain equipment and facilities used for sterile drug manufacturing. This is a violation of 21 CFR 211.42(c).

The inspection documented damaged ceiling panels and unsealed penetrations above the ISO 7 staging area.

These violations cause your drug products to be adulterated within the meaning of section 501(a)(2)(B) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.
