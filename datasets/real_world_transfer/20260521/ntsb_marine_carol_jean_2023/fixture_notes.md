# Fixture Notes — ntsb_marine_carol_jean_2023

## Document type
NTSB Marine Investigation Report (MIR), final factual and probable-cause report.

## Issuing organization
National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB), in coordination with the U.S. Coast Guard Marine Safety Unit Savannah.

## General subject matter
On March 21, 2023, the wooden-hulled 71.9-foot commercial fishing vessel *Carol Jean* sank in the Atlantic Ocean about 13 miles east of Tybee Island, Georgia, while anchored and unattended. The events leading up to the sinking began on March 14 when the vessel set out from Valona, Georgia, to acquire and tow a second fishing vessel, *Having Faith*, back from Port Royal, South Carolina. A tow-line failure, propeller fouling, helicopter rescue, partial diver repair, and eventual sinking unfold over an eight-day timeline.
