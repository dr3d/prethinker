Summarized Board Decisions:
1. SAAS Hotels NJ LLC d/b/a La Quinta Inn & Suites Fairfield and Rollo Hospitality LLC d/b/a Ramada by Wyndham, Alter Egos (22-CA-315658 and 22-CA-323960; 374 NLRB No. 99) Fairfield NJ April 22 2026
   - Default Judgment granted as to Rollo for failure to file answer; violations Section 8(a)(3) and (1) and Section 8(a)(5) and (1)
   - Denied without prejudice as to SAAS Hotels NJ LLC (GC failed to prove effective service)

2. BeLeaf Medical, LLC (14-RC-325871, 374 NLRB No. 100) St. Louis MO April 23 2026
   - Employer's Request for Review denied
   - "Post Harvest" classifications not "agricultural" under FLSA def. used for NLRA section 2(3) exclusion
   - Member Mayer concurred: case-by-case approach especially in "emerging or rapidly industries"
   - Petitioner: UFCW Local 655
