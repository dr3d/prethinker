Identified risk-factor categories include the risk that additional information may arise out of the continuing review of the financial statements and the evaluation of accounting practices, the preparation of the Company's restated financial statements or other subsequent events that would require the Company to make additional adjustments, as well as inherent limitations in internal control over financial reporting.
