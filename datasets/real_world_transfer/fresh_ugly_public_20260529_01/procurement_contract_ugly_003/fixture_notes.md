## Why this document is messy

It layers: a multi-firm counsel block (three firms, eight attorneys); a three-item Digest mixing dismissal-as-untimely with denials on the merits; a background with two separate decision histories (the prior B-423552/.2 sustained-in-part decision and the current B-423552.3 decision); a quantitative evaluation comparison table; multiple procedural deadlines and CFR cites; a doctrinal distinguishing analysis of an earlier GAO case (eAlliant, LLC); a sub-footnote about a textual omission in the protester's reading of GAO's prior recommendation; and a precise tradeoff math conclusion.

- The three "dot" variants of the file number (B-423552, B-423552.2, B-423552.3) refer to three distinct protest filings under a single matter.
- The prior recommendation language reads in full: "reevaluate proposals in a manner consistent with the terms of the solicitation and this decision" — but the protester's excerpt omitted "and this decision," and the precise scope of the recommendation depends on that omitted phrase.
