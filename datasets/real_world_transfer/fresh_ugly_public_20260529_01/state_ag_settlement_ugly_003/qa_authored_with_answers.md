# QA with reference answers — state_ag_settlement_ugly_003

Source: NY AG Assurance of Discontinuance, In the Matter of Equifax Information Services LLC, AOD 24-102 (effective January 2, 2025).

q001. What is the Assurance of Discontinuance number, and what is the effective date of the Assurance?
Answer: AOD 24-102; effective date January 2, 2025.

q002. Who is the New York Attorney General named in the caption of this matter?
Answer: Letitia James, Attorney General of the State of New York.

q003. Which two NYAG signatories signed for the NYAG, and what are their respective titles?
Answer: Jane Azia, Bureau Chief, Consumer Frauds & Protection Bureau; and Glenna Goldis, Assistant Attorney General, Consumer Frauds & Protection Bureau.

q004. What is Equifax's full corporate name and its state of formation/type?
Answer: Equifax Information Services LLC; a for-profit Georgia limited liability company.

q005. What is Equifax's principal place of business address?
Answer: 1550 Peachtree Street, N.W., Atlanta, Georgia 30309.

q006. Who is Equifax's registered agent in New York, and at what address?
Answer: The Prentice-Hall Corporation System, Inc., located at 80 State Street, Albany NY 12207.

q007. Who signed the Assurance for Equifax, and what is the signatory's title?
Answer: Nick Oldham, Chief Compliance Officer, Equifax Information Services LLC.

q008. Who is identified as Counsel for Equifax (attorney, firm, and firm address)?
Answer: John C. Redding, Alston & Bird LLP, 1120 South Tryon Street, Ste. 300, Charlotte, NC 28203-6818.

q009. On what date was the code change introduced to Equifax's Online Model Server (the "OMS Issue")?
Answer: March 17, 2022.

q010. On what date did Equifax initiate its investigation into the OMS Issue?
Answer: March 22, 2022.

q011. On what date did Equifax partially resolve the OMS Issue, and on what date did it fully resolve it?
Answer: Partially resolved April 6, 2022; fully resolved April 8, 2022.

q012. Within how many days after the date of the Assurance must Equifax pay the monetary relief?
Answer: Within thirty (30) days after the date of this Assurance.

q013. Place these 2022 events in chronological order: investigation initiated; OMS Issue code change introduced; partial resolution; full resolution.
Answer: (1) OMS Issue code change introduced March 17, 2022; (2) investigation initiated March 22, 2022; (3) partial resolution April 6, 2022; (4) full resolution April 8, 2022.

q014. How frequently must Equifax monitor incident reports from customers under paragraph 13?
Answer: Regularly, but in no event less than once per week.

q015. Under what New York statutory provisions did the NYAG commence its investigation?
Answer: Executive Law § 63(12) and Article 22-A of the General Business Law ("GBL") §§ 349-350.

q016. Under paragraph 5, which laws must Equifax prospectively comply with in connection with code used to calculate score values of New Yorkers and representations to New York consumers about score values?
Answer: Executive Law § 63(12); GBL §§ 349, 350, and 380-j; and 15 U.S.C. 1681 et seq., and any further amendments to those laws, regulations, and rules adopted subsequent to the date of the Assurance.

q017. What is the amount of monetary relief Equifax must pay, and under what GBL provision?
Answer: $725,000, pursuant to GBL §§ 349-d, to be used as restitution and penalties as deemed appropriate in the sole and absolute discretion of the NYAG.

q018. What specific subsections of the FCRA and the NYFCRA does paragraph 6 require Equifax to comply with?
Answer: FCRA: 15 U.S.C. § 1681e(b). NYFCRA: General Business Law § 380-j(a).

q019. Approximately how many credit-seeking New York consumers did Equifax estimate experienced a negative score shift as a result of the OMS Issue?
Answer: Approximately 76,000.

q020. In what numbered paragraph is the no-admission of negligence, wrongdoing, or violation of law set out?
Answer: Paragraph 17.

q021. What does paragraph 26 provide regarding the consequence if any material representations by Equifax or its counsel are later found to be inaccurate or misleading?
Answer: The Assurance is voidable by the NYAG in its sole discretion.

q022. Which numbered paragraph contains the counterparts clause, and what does it provide?
Answer: Paragraph 36; the Assurance may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed to be an original, but all of which, taken together, shall constitute one and the same agreement.

q023. Under paragraph 14, what release does the NYAG provide regarding the OMS Issue?
Answer: The NYAG shall release and discharge Equifax from all claims that the NYAG could have brought based on the OMS Issue. Nothing in paragraph 14 will limit the NYAG's ability to enforce obligations Equifax has under the AOD.

q024. If Equifax requests that information be excepted from disclosure under Public Officers Law § 87, what procedures shall the NYAG follow?
Answer: The NYAG shall follow the procedures delineated in section 89(5) of the Public Officers Law.

q025. Default in the performance of obligations under which range of paragraphs is acknowledged as a violation of the Assurance, and what does the NYAG thereafter have a right to do?
Answer: Default in the performance of any obligation under paragraphs 6 through 14 is a violation of the Assurance; the NYAG thereafter may commence a civil action or proceeding contemplated in paragraph 1, supra, in addition to any other appropriate investigation, action, or proceeding.
