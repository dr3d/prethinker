ATTORNEY GENERAL OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK
BUREAU OF CONSUMER FRAUDS & PROTECTION

In the Matter of the Investigation by Letitia James, Attorney General of New York, of Equifax Information Services LLC, Respondent.
AOD 24-102
ASSURANCE OF DISCONTINUANCE

Investigation commenced under Executive Law § 63(12) and GBL Article 22-A (§§ 349-350).

FINDINGS:
- Equifax = for-profit Georgia LLC; principal place of business 1550 Peachtree Street, N.W., Atlanta, GA 30309.
- Registered agent in NY: The Prentice-Hall Corporation System, Inc., 80 State Street, Albany NY 12207.
- "OMS Issue": code change to Online Model Server on March 17, 2022.
- Equifax initiated investigation March 22, 2022; partially resolved April 6, 2022; fully resolved April 8, 2022.
- ~76,000 credit-seeking NY consumers experienced negative score shifts.
- Unknown number of additional NY consumers had affected "soft" credit inquiries not included in Equifax's calculation.
- Equifax's representations of consistent-formula calculation were incorrect while OMS Issue was operating.

AGREEMENT (39 paragraphs total):

- Developer training module on FCRA accuracy.
- Weekly monitoring of incident reports for adverse-impact issues.
- Cooperate with NYAG info requests re OMS Issue; Public Officers Law § 87, § 89(5) procedures.
- NYAG release & discharge re OMS Issue.
