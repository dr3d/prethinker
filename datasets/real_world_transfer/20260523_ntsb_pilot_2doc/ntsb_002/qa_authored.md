q001: What was the name of the towing vessel that capsized?

q002: What was the date of the casualty?

q003: What barge was being towed at the time of the incident?

q004: How many crewmembers were aboard the Baylor J. Tregre?

q005: In what year was the Baylor J. Tregre originally built?

## Timeline and Date Inference Questions

q006: At what time did the vessel depart from Houma?

q007: When did the mate notice the squall forming?

q008: At what time did the mate make the distress call?

q009: How many minutes elapsed between the vessel losing electrical power and the last AIS transmission?

q010: At what time did the Coast Guard recover all four crew members?

## Actor and Action Attribution Questions

q012: Which crew member was the first to notice the vessel heeling 45° to port?

## Causal Chain Questions

q014: What factor prevented the mate from releasing the tow when instructed to do so?

## Count and Table Extraction Questions

q018: What was the estimated property damage to the Baylor J. Tregre?

## Contradiction or Competing Explanation Questions

q020: What conflicting evidence exists regarding whether Special Marine Warnings reached the vessel?

## Unresolved or Open Issues
