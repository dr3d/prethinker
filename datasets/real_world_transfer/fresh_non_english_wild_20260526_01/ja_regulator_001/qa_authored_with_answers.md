**Q12 [LT, EN/EN]** What is the literal meaning of "銀証連携ビジネス" (gin-shō renkei business), and why is it the regulatory pain point in this case?
**Answer:** "銀証連携ビジネス" literally means "bank-securities collaboration business" — the integrated business model where a bank and its securities-firm sister entity (within the same financial group) jointly serve corporate and high-net-worth customers, sharing information and cross-selling products. The regulatory pain point is that Japanese banking law prohibits banks from engaging in non-bank businesses (他業禁止, art. 12) and requires segregation of customer information across legal entities (art. 12-2 para 2); the FSA found 銀証連携 practices violating both. The English translation in FSA materials is "bank-securities collaboration".
**Coordinates:** 違反内容の説明 + FSA英文資料.

**Q18 [OV, JA/JA]** 業務改善命令により三菱UFJ銀行および三菱UFJモルガン・スタンレー証券が取らねばならない義務を述べてください。

**Q19 [OV, JA/JA]** 銀行法 第12条 (他業禁止) と 第12条の2 第2項 (顧客情報管理) は、銀証連携の文脈でどのように違反となりますか。
**回答:** 第12条 (他業禁止) は銀行が銀行業以外の業務を営むことを原則として禁止する規定。銀証連携ビジネスにおいて銀行が証券業務に実質的に関与することは、銀行業の枠を超え他業禁止に抵触する可能性がある。第12条の2第2項 (顧客情報管理) は、銀行が顧客情報を適切に管理し、グループ内の他法人(証券子会社等)へ不当に共有・利用することを規制する規定。法令違反となるのは、顧客の同意なく顧客情報を証券会社と共有した場合、もしくは顧客情報を銀行業以外の目的(証券営業等)で利用した場合。
**ソース箇所:** 違反内容の法的説明部。

**Q20 [OV, EN/EN]** Why is the FSA's 業務改善命令 issued to two subsidiary entities (the bank and the securities firm), while the parent holding (MUFG) receives only a 報告徴求?
**Answer:** Because the operational violations occurred at the two operating subsidiaries — 三菱UFJ銀行 conducted the inappropriate sharing of customer information, and 三菱UFJモルガン・スタンレー証券 received and used that information. The FSA directly orders these two to take 改善 measures. MUFG, the parent holding, is not the operational counterpart but is responsible for group governance; the 報告徴求 to MUFG under banking law 第52条の31第1項 demands information on group-level oversight and remediation, but does not directly command operational change. This is consistent with the standard Japanese regulatory approach of directly ordering operational entities while gathering information from holdcos.
**Coordinates:** プレスリリース本文構造 + 銀行法 第52条の31第1項.

**Q23 [CN, EN/EN]** Did the FSA impose any monetary penalty (課徴金/罰金) in this case alongside the 業務改善命令?
**Answer:** No. The FSA's action consists of (1) 業務改善命令 (administrative improvement order) and (2) 報告徴求 (report-demand). Neither is a monetary penalty. The Japanese regulatory framework treats 業務改善命令 as a structural / behavioural remedy, distinct from 課徴金 (administrative surcharge) which requires a separate process (typically initiated by SESC for FIEA violations and adjudicated through the 金融庁 surcharge tribunal). No 課徴金 is mentioned in this press release.
**Coordinates:** プレスリリース全体 — 処分の種類.

**Q24 [CN, EN/EN]** Did the FSA's order require any specific personnel changes (e.g., dismissal or role changes) at MUFG?
**Answer:** No. The press release does NOT mandate specific personnel actions. The 業務改善命令 requires structural and procedural remediation but leaves personnel decisions to the parent holding and the subsidiaries themselves. In practice, MUFG's subsequent voluntary disclosures may include role-change announcements, but those are not commanded by the FSA order.
