## Dates, deadlines, durations, sequence

16. What is the sequence of events the release describes, from drum explosion to citation issuance?

    Sequence per the release text:
    1. A 55-gallon fiber drum containing nitrocellulose explodes (lede).
    2. The explosion starts a secondary fire involving flammable liquids (lede).
    3. OSHA initiates an inspection (paragraph 2).
    4. OSHA finds the employer lacks a new-hire safety and health program and failed to provide chemical and respiratory protection training, fit testing, and medical evaluations (paragraph 2).
    5. OSHA cites the employer for two repeat, ten serious, and six other-than-serious violations and proposes $275,694 in penalties (paragraph 3).
