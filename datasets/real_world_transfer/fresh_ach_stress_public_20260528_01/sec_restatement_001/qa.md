# QA — sec_restatement_001

All answers are contained entirely within source.md. No outside knowledge of SEC rules, accounting standards, or the company is required beyond what the 8-K states.

---

**Q1 [direct_fact]** What action did the Board conclude to take on November 4, 2024?
**A:** To amend the FY2023 Form 10-K and the Q1 and Q2 2024 Form 10-Qs to restate the segment information disclosure, because the prior filings should no longer be relied upon due to identified errors.
**Coords:** [ITEM 4.02 — TRIGGER AND SCOPE]

**Q2 [direct_fact]** Which specific disclosures/notes are being restated, and for which periods?
**A:** The segment information disclosure — Note 17 in the FY2023 Form 10-K and Note 13 in the Q1 and Q2 2024 Form 10-Qs — covering fiscal years 2021, 2022, and 2023, and the quarterly and year-to-date periods in the Q1 and Q2 2024 Form 10-Qs.
**Coords:** [ITEM 4.02 — TRIGGER AND SCOPE]

**Q3 [evidence_extraction]** What kind of error is at the center of the restatement?
**A:** Misclassified intersegment transactions — sales that included intrasegment sales (within a segment) but should have included exclusively intersegment sales (from one segment to another), plus some transactions inconsistent with revenue recognition and segment reporting standards that should not have been reported as intersegment sales.
**Coords:** [PRIOR-CORRECTED ERRORS AND MATERIAL WEAKNESS]; [NEWLY IDENTIFIED ERRORS — DISCOVERED DURING REMEDIATION TESTING]

**Q4 [evidence_extraction]** How were the newly identified errors discovered?
**A:** In the course of testing new controls implemented as part of the Company's material weakness remediation plan in the third quarter of 2024.
**Coords:** [NEWLY IDENTIFIED ERRORS — DISCOVERED DURING REMEDIATION TESTING]

**Q5 [competing_cause]** What does the Company itself identify as the underlying control condition behind the intersegment-sales errors?
**A:** A material weakness in its internal control over financial reporting related to its accounting practices and procedures for intersegment sales.
**Coords:** [PRIOR-CORRECTED ERRORS AND MATERIAL WEAKNESS]

**Q6 [competing_cause]** What is the stated impact of the restatements on the consolidated primary financial statements?
**A:** The restatements are not expected to result in any material impact on the Consolidated Statements of Earnings, Comprehensive Income (Loss), Balance Sheets, Cash Flow, or Shareholders' Equity for the periods presented.
**Coords:** [IMPACT STATEMENT]

**Q7 [competing_cause]** What role did the SEC play in the timing of the Board's conclusion, per the document?
**A:** The Board reached its conclusion "following the Company's ongoing dialogue with the staff of the United States Securities and Exchange Commission." The document ties the timing to that dialogue but does not state the SEC dictated the accounting conclusion.
**Coords:** [ITEM 4.02 — TRIGGER AND SCOPE]

**Q8 [competing_cause]** Was the material weakness newly discovered in this 8-K, or previously disclosed?
**A:** Previously disclosed. The material weakness was identified in connection with earlier error corrections and disclosed in the FY2023 Form 10-K and the Q1 and Q2 2024 Form 10-Qs, with a remediation plan already in place.
**Coords:** [PRIOR-CORRECTED ERRORS AND MATERIAL WEAKNESS]; [REMEDIATION STATUS]

**Q9 [competing_cause]** What remediation steps does the Company describe?
**A:** Enhancing the design and documentation of pricing and measurement/reporting controls for segment-disclosure purposes and projected financial information used in impairment analyses, with continued testing through the balance of the year; and providing ongoing training for relevant personnel on measuring intersegment sales and applying relevant accounting guidance.
**Coords:** [REMEDIATION STATUS]

**Q10 [pattern_evidence]** Is the restatement a one-time correction or part of a recurring pattern of the same type of error? Support with the text.
**A:** A recurring pattern of the same type of error. The Company had already identified and corrected certain intersegment-sales misclassifications (disclosed in the FY2023 10-K), then identified additional misclassified intersegment transactions across multiple segments while testing remediation controls — the same intrasegment-vs-intersegment issue recurring.
**Coords:** [PRIOR-CORRECTED ERRORS AND MATERIAL WEAKNESS]; [NEWLY IDENTIFIED ERRORS — DISCOVERED DURING REMEDIATION TESTING]

**Q11 [sensitivity]** If the impact statement (no material effect on the consolidated statements) were removed, would the best explanation for the restatement change? Use only the document.
**A:** No. Removing the no-material-impact statement would weaken the "contained reclassification" reading, but the best explanation — a material weakness in internal control over intersegment-sales reporting — is still supported by the identification of the material weakness, its discovery of additional errors during remediation testing, and its ongoing remediation. The winner would not change; it would, if anything, strengthen.
**Coords:** [IMPACT STATEMENT]; [PRIOR-CORRECTED ERRORS AND MATERIAL WEAKNESS]; [REMEDIATION STATUS]

**Q12 [sensitivity]** If the reference to SEC-staff dialogue were removed, would the restatement be best explained as regulator-driven? Use only the document.
**A:** No. The SEC-staff-dialogue reference is the only support for a regulator-driven reading; removing it removes that explanation entirely. The document would still attribute the errors to a material weakness in internal control over intersegment-sales reporting.
**Coords:** [ITEM 4.02 — TRIGGER AND SCOPE]; [PRIOR-CORRECTED ERRORS AND MATERIAL WEAKNESS]

**Q13 [negation]** Does the document state that the restatement will change the Company's reported net earnings for any period?
**A:** No. It states the restatements are not expected to result in any material impact on the Consolidated Statements of Earnings (or the other consolidated primary statements).
**Coords:** [IMPACT STATEMENT]

**Q14 [premise_check]** Is it correct that the restatement was triggered by errors in the Company's consolidated balance sheet?
**A:** No. The restatement concerns the segment information disclosure (Note 17 / Note 13). The document states the restatements are not expected to materially affect the consolidated balance sheets or other consolidated primary statements.
**Coords:** [ITEM 4.02 — TRIGGER AND SCOPE]; [IMPACT STATEMENT]

**Q15 [premise_check]** Is it correct that the material weakness was first identified through the SEC staff's review?
**A:** No. The material weakness was identified by the Company in connection with its own earlier error corrections and was disclosed in the FY2023 Form 10-K and Q1 and Q2 2024 Form 10-Qs. The 8-K ties only the timing of the Board's restatement conclusion to ongoing SEC-staff dialogue.
**Coords:** [PRIOR-CORRECTED ERRORS AND MATERIAL WEAKNESS]; [ITEM 4.02 — TRIGGER AND SCOPE]
