# QA

Q1. Who is the registrant and what type of filing is this?
Expected: Aurora Acquisition Corp.; a Form 8-K/A (Amendment No. 1), a current report under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.

Q2. What is the date of the report and the date of the earliest event reported?
Expected: Date of report February 10, 2022; date of earliest event reported March 8, 2021.

Q3. What is the registrant's jurisdiction of incorporation and commission file number?
Expected: Cayman Islands; Commission File Number 001-40143.

Q4. What document is being amended and restated, and when was the original filed?
Expected: The audited balance sheet as of March 8, 2021, filed as Exhibit 99.1 to the Company's original Form 8-K, which was filed with the SEC on March 12, 2021.

Q5. What was the first category of error identified?
Expected: Warrants were initially presented as components of equity but should have been classified as a liability; related offering costs were initially recorded as a reduction of equity, and a portion should have been expensed immediately.

Q6. How were the Class A shares subject to redemption restated, and under what guidance?
Expected: They were restated to full redemption value in accordance with ASC 480-10-S99, in light of subsequent SEC Staff communications.

Q7. What was the third error, concerning the underwriters' option?
Expected: The underwriters' 45-day over-allotment option to purchase up to 3,300,000 additional Units should have been classified as a liability pursuant to ASC 480.

Q8. Who concluded that the financial statements contained errors?
Expected: The Audit Committee of the Board of Directors, after discussion with the Company's management (and in agreement with the Company's independent accountants).

Q9. What did the Company say about the effect of the restatement on cash?
Expected: The Company does not expect these changes will have any impact on its cash position and the cash held in the trust account established in connection with its IPO.

Q10. What did management conclude about internal controls?
Expected: That in light of the errors, a material weakness exists in internal control over financial reporting and that disclosure controls and procedures were not effective.

Q11. Where is the material weakness discussed in more detail?
Expected: In Part I, Item 4, "Controls and Procedures," of the Company's Form 10-Q for the period ended September 30, 2021, filed with the SEC on November 15, 2021.

Q12. What exhibits are listed in Item 9.01?
Expected: Exhibit 99.1 (Audited Balance Sheet, as of March 8, 2021) and Exhibit 104 (Cover Page Interactive Data File embedded within the Inline XBRL document).

Q13. Does the Amendment change any other disclosures in the original Form 8-K?
Expected: No; except as described, the Amendment does not amend, update, or change any other disclosures in the Original Form 8-K and does not reflect events occurring after the original filing.

Q14. Who signed the Amendment and in what capacity?
Expected: Arnaud Massenet, Chief Executive Officer of Aurora Acquisition Corp.

Q15. On what exchange were the Company's securities registered, and what is the warrant exercise price?
Expected: The NASDAQ Stock Market LLC; each whole redeemable warrant is exercisable for one Class A Ordinary Share at an exercise price of $11.50.
