# Fixture notes — puc_order_ugly_002

## Why this document is messy
It is a California Public Utilities Commission enforcement resolution that resolves a contested Administrative Enforcement Order via a negotiated settlement. It layers a routing-coded caption, a summary, a procedural background spanning many dated filings, recited settlement terms, a five-factor penalty analysis quoting an enforcement-policy attachment, a twelve-item numbered Findings list, a six-paragraph numbered Order with payment mechanics, a commissioner vote block (including a recusal), and an Appendix reference to a separate settlement document.
