# QA with reference answers — sec_material_event_ugly_006

Source: SEC Form 8-K, Cable One, Inc., Date of Report December 31, 2025 (Items 5.02, 7.01, 9.01).

q001. What is the registrant's name and its state of incorporation?
Answer: Cable One, Inc.; Delaware. [COVER PAGE]

q002. What are the Commission File Number and the I.R.S. Employer Identification Number, exactly as shown?
Answer: Commission File Number 001-36863; I.R.S. Employer Identification No. 13-3060083. [COVER PAGE]

q003. What is the address of the principal executive offices, including zip code?
Answer: 210 E. Earll Drive, Phoenix, Arizona 85012. [COVER PAGE]

q004. What is the trading symbol, the exchange, and the par value of the common stock?
Answer: Trading symbol CABO; New York Stock Exchange; par value $0.01 per share. [COVER PAGE]

q005. Who signed the report, and what is that person's title?
Answer: Christopher J. Arntzen, Senior Vice President, General Counsel and Secretary. [SIGNATURE]

q006. Who was appointed Chief Executive Officer, and what is his age?
Answer: James A. Holanda, age 60. [ITEM 5.02]

q007. Who was appointed Interim Chief Executive Officer, what is his existing role, and what is his age?
Answer: Todd M. Koetje, the Company's Chief Financial Officer; age 49. [ITEM 5.02]

q008. Who was elected Chair of the Board?
Answer: Mary E. Meduski. [ITEM 5.02]

q009. What is the Date of Report (Date of Earliest Event Reported)?
Answer: December 31, 2025. [COVER PAGE]

q010. When is Holanda's appointment as Chief Executive Officer effective?
Answer: Effective no later than March 31, 2026 (defined as the "Commencement Date"). [ITEM 5.02]

q011. When is Meduski's election as Chair of the Board effective?
Answer: January 1, 2026. [ITEM 5.02]

q012. Over what period does Koetje serve as Interim Chief Executive Officer?
Answer: From January 1, 2026 through the Commencement Date. [ITEM 5.02]

q013. What is the date of the Offer Letter, and when does it become effective?
Answer: Dated December 23, 2025; effective as of the Commencement Date. [ITEM 5.02]/[ITEM 9.01 exhibit 10.1]

q014. When does Holanda's eligibility for annual equity-based award grants begin?
Answer: Beginning January 1, 2027. [ITEM 5.02]

q015. Since when has Koetje been Chief Financial Officer, and since when has Meduski been a Board member?
Answer: Koetje has been CFO since July 2022; Meduski has been a Board member since 2019. [ITEM 5.02]

q016. List the three exhibits with their descriptions and dates.
Answer: Exhibit 10.1 — Offer Letter dated December 23, 2025; Exhibit 99.1 — press release issued by Cable One, Inc. dated December 31, 2025; Exhibit 104 — the cover page formatted in Inline XBRL. [ITEM 9.01]

q017. Which numbered Items of Form 8-K does this report address?
Answer: Item 5.02; Item 7.01 (Regulation FD Disclosure); and Item 9.01. [ITEM 5.02]/[ITEM 7.01]/[ITEM 9.01]

q018. What are the components of Holanda's compensation and benefits under the Offer Letter?
Answer: (i) annual base salary $1,400,000; (ii) annual target bonus equal to 150% of base salary; (iii) a one-time equity grant with aggregate grant-date fair value of approximately $10,000,000 (40% time-based RSUs, 60% performance-based RSUs); (iv) beginning January 1, 2027, eligibility for annual equity-based award grants; and (v) a one-time $175,000 relocation cash payment; plus up to $750,000 to replace a "Foregone Bonus." [ITEM 5.02]

q019. Which committee approved Holanda's compensation?
Answer: The Compensation and Talent Management Committee of the Board. [ITEM 5.02]

q020. Which committee does Meduski chair, and on which other committee does she sit?
Answer: She chairs the Nominating and Governance Committee (a role she retains as Chair of the Board) and is a member of the Executive Committee. [ITEM 5.02]

q021. Are there any family relationships or selection arrangements involving Holanda?
Answer: No. There are no family relationships (as defined in Item 401(d) of Regulation S-K) and no arrangement or understanding under which he was selected as CEO. [ITEM 5.02]

q022. Under what condition must Holanda refund the Foregone Bonus payment?
Answer: If his employment with the Company ends for any reason — other than certain involuntary terminations by the Company or his resignation for "good reason" — less than two years after the Commencement Date. [ITEM 5.02]

q023. How is the Item 7.01 information (and Exhibit 99.1) characterized for liability purposes?
Answer: It is "furnished" and shall not be deemed to be "filed" for purposes of Section 18 of the Exchange Act, nor incorporated by reference into other filings, except as expressly set forth by specific reference. [ITEM 7.01]

q024. Julia M. Laulis held which roles, and how are those roles reallocated among the named individuals?
Answer: Laulis retired from her roles as Chair of the Board, President, and Chief Executive Officer; Holanda becomes Chief Executive Officer (and a Board member), Meduski becomes Chair of the Board, and Koetje serves as Interim Chief Executive Officer through the Commencement Date. [ITEM 5.02] (succession + appointments)

q025. Why is Koetje's Interim CEO term tied to "the Commencement Date," and what does that date represent?
Answer: Koetje serves as Interim CEO from January 1, 2026 "through the Commencement Date," which is the date Holanda's CEO appointment becomes effective (no later than March 31, 2026); the interim term bridges the gap until Holanda can complete his prior professional commitments and commence employment. [ITEM 5.02] (Koetje term + Commencement Date definition)
