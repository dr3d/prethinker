# QA with reference answers — state_ag_settlement_ugly_002

Source: NY Attorney General Assurance of Discontinuance, In re Equinox Group, LLC / SoulCycle, Assurance No. 24-099 (OAG Findings).

q001. What is the Assurance number, which Attorney General brought it, and through which bureau?
Answer: Assurance No. 24-099; brought by Letitia James, Attorney General of the State of New York, through the Bureau of Internet and Technology. (para [CAPTION])

q002. Who are the four named Respondents?
Answer: Equinox Group, LLC; Equinox Holdings, Inc.; Equinox Media, LLC; and SoulCycle LLC. (para [CAPTION])

q003. What is Equinox Group's corporate form and the address of its principal place of business?
Answer: A Delaware corporation; its principal place of business is at 31 Hudson Yards, New York, NY 10001. (para [BACKGROUND] 13)

q004. Which Equinox Group subsidiary filed for bankruptcy, and on what date?
Answer: Blink Fitness; it filed for bankruptcy on August 12, 2024. (para [BACKGROUND] 15)

q005. Under what statutes did the OAG commence its investigation?
Answer: Executive Law § 63(12); General Business Law §§ 349, 350, and 527-a; and the Restore Online Shoppers' Confidence Act (ROSCA), 15 U.S.C. § 8403(3). (para [CAPTION])

q006. How many Equinox gyms are in New York state, and how does that compare with other states?
Answer: There are currently 43 Equinox gyms in New York state — more than in any other state. (para [I. EQUINOX ENROLLMENT] 17)

q007. What is the address of SoulCycle's headquarters, as it appears in the cancellation notice?
Answer: SoulCycle Inc., 609 Greenwich Street, New York, New York, 10014. (para [II. SOULCYCLE] 58/59-67)

q008. Over what period did Respondents offer the violating automatically renewing memberships through Equinox and Equinox Media?
Answer: Since at least February 9, 2021 and until at least July 28, 2024. (para [BACKGROUND] 16)

q009. When did SoulCycle begin offering its automatically renewing membership (Soul Renew)?
Answer: September 2021. (para [BACKGROUND] 16 / [II. SOULCYCLE] 57)

q010. When did Equinox establish an online cancellation option for all New York members?
Answer: Beginning July 28, 2024. (para [EQUINOX APP PT/PILATES] 47-54)

q011. On what day of each month were Equinox monthly membership fees charged?
Answer: On the 23rd day of each month. (para [I. EQUINOX ENROLLMENT] 22)

q012. How many days of notice were Equinox Members required to provide to cancel their memberships?
Answer: At least 45 days of notice. (para [I. EQUINOX ENROLLMENT] 22)

q013. When did Equinox stop using webchat cancellation?
Answer: March 2023. (para [EQUINOX CANCELLATION] 39-45)

q014. The Findings recite the same three-part set of violations for Equinox, SoulCycle, and the Equinox+ App. What are the three violations, and which legal provision is cited?
Answer: (i) failing to present the material terms of the offer clearly and conspicuously before the subscription was fulfilled; (ii) failing to obtain the consumer's affirmative consent to the agreement containing the automatic renewal offer terms; and (iii) failing to provide a cost-effective, timely, and easy-to-use mechanism for cancellation. GBL § 527-a(1)-(2) (and ROSCA, 15 U.S.C. § 8403). (para [I.] 18 / [II.] 56 / [III.] 78)

q015. List the four circumstances under which an Equinox Member could cancel without penalty during the obligation period.
Answer: (i) a physical disability that would prevent use of Equinox's services for more than six months; (ii) moving more than 25 miles away from any Equinox location; (iii) Equinox could no longer provide the contracted services (e.g., their location closed); and (iv) death, in which case the estate could cancel. (para [I. EQUINOX ENROLLMENT] 22)

q016. What were the three Equinox membership tiers, and what did each offer?
Answer: "Select" (access to only a single gym); "All-Access" (access to all U.S. Equinox locations); and "Destination" (access to Equinox locations worldwide). (para [I. EQUINOX ENROLLMENT] 19)

q017. Per GBL § 527(3), what does "clear and conspicuous" mean?
Answer: "In larger type than the surrounding text, or in contrasting type, font, or color to the surrounding text of the same size, or set off from the surrounding text of the same size by symbols or other marks, in a manner that clearly calls attention to the language." (para [LEGAL FRAMEWORK] 4)

q018. Under New York GBL § 624(2), within how many days of joining could New York members cancel their memberships without penalty?
Answer: Within three days of joining. (para [I. EQUINOX ENROLLMENT] 22(h))

q019. Identify at least three material terms of a Soul Renew membership stated in the Findings.
Answer: Among the material terms: the classes expired after 30 days and did not roll over into the next month; the classes were non-transferable; and a Member had to cancel at least 24 hours prior to the next billing cycle to avoid being charged for the following month (others include the price depending on number of classes, automatic monthly renewal until cancellation, SoulCycle's reserved right to incrementally increase the price upon advance written notice, and the billing date). (para [II. SOULCYCLE] 58)

q020. What did Equinox's online enrollment flow fail to obtain from the consumer at any point, in violation of the law?
Answer: The consumer's express affirmative consent to the negative option feature of the membership. (para [I. EQUINOX ENROLLMENT] 23-29)

q021. How and when was the Equinox initiation fee disclosed during online enrollment, and what was the problem?
Answer: The initiation fee (which could be as high as $300) was disclosed for the first time on the payment page, only after the consumer had navigated three other pages — and after the enrollment flow had already obtained the consumer's consent to be contacted by a Membership Advisor. (para [I. EQUINOX ENROLLMENT] 23-29)

q022. When an Equinox Member tried to cancel, what was a manager directed to do, and what were managers instructed not to do?
Answer: Managers were directed to "TRY TO SAVE THE MEMBER" (e.g., offer to freeze or transfer the membership) and were instructed that they should not actively inform Members that they might be able to cancel without further charge in certain circumstances; the instruction stated that if a member is suffering from a medical condition or relocating, "this is something that will come up in conversation and is not something we need to offer." (para [EQUINOX CANCELLATION] 38)

q023. Which Equinox cancellation mechanism was not publicized in the membership documents or FAQs?
Answer: The online webchat cancellation option (cancellation via webchat with an Equinox Concierge) — it was not included in the membership documents or FAQs. (para [EQUINOX CANCELLATION] 33-34/39-45)

q024. How did the burden of Equinox's webchat cancellation process compare with its enrollment flow?
Answer: The webchat cancellation required the Member to respond to between 1 and 4 automated prompts, wait to connect to a Concierge, and respond to at least one "save" attempt before the cancellation process would begin — significantly more burdensome than the enrollment flow, which required navigating through just three pages and guided the consumer with prominent visual and textual cues. (para [EQUINOX CANCELLATION] 39-45 + [enrollment] 23-29)

q025. For SoulCycle's January 2023 Soul Renew promotion (code SOULRENEW20), what material term of the promotion was not disclosed, and where was it omitted?
Answer: That the 20% discount applied only to the consumer's initial purchase and that subsequent packages would automatically renew at the higher, standard price (i.e., when the promotion would end). This fact was not disclosed anywhere — not in the promotional offer, not in the online purchase flow, and not in the terms and conditions that appeared in the purchase flow. (para [II. SOULCYCLE] 68 + 66)
