# Provenance — fda_warning_ugly_001

- **Source URL:** https://www.fda.gov/inspections-compliance-enforcement-and-criminal-investigations/warning-letters/medical-products-laboratories-inc-721916-04092026
- **Retrieval date:** 2026-05-24
- **Document format (original):** HTML page on fda.gov
- **Transformation performed:**
    - Page text was extracted from the published FDA warning letter.
    - `source_original.txt` is a plain-text capture of the letter body (recipient block, issuing office, all four CGMP violation sections, unapproved-new-drug section, signatures, footnote).
    - `source.md` is a Markdown rendering of the same content. Headings, signature block, bulleted response items, two-column delivery/product field block, and the (b)(4) redactions of the original are preserved.
    - FDA site chrome (nav, breadcrumbs, footer links, "More Warning Letters") removed.
- **Excerpt boundaries:** `source.md` is the **full text** of the published warning letter, not an excerpt. The "Content current as of: 04/14/2026" and "Regulated Product(s): Drugs" metadata footer that FDA appends to the published page is retained at the bottom.
- **Caveats:**
    - The published letter contains numerous (b)(4) FOIA redactions; these are preserved verbatim as `(b)(4)`. Specific lot identifiers, product names involved in microbiological failures, the (b)(4) treatment process, the customer name, recall dates, and stability temperature numerics are therefore not present in the source and cannot be recovered from it.
    - The signature lines render as "/S/" on the FDA page; the underlying signed names (Francis Godwin, Tina Smith) and titles are printed below each `/S/` line.
