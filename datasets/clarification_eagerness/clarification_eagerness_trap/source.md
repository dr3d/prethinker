## Charter rules

### Charter Rule 2: Filing deadline

A repair permit is timely if the permit is filed within two business days after the inspection record is logged. If an emergency certificate is attached, the permit may be filed within three business days after the inspection record is logged.

### Charter Rule 5: Safety suspension

If the wake gauge exceeds 18 twice within a 24-hour period, ferry operation is suspended. The suspension remains active until the Harbor Master or Acting Harbor Master lifts it after a follow-up inspection.

### Charter Rule 7: Procurement release

A repair payment may be released only if the Harbor Clerk countersigns the repair packet and the Treasurer releases the funds. The Contractor Lead's signature alone does not authorize payment.

### Charter Rule 9: Claim boundary

A witness statement, staff memo, minority report, or contractor letter is a claim unless the Dock Review Panel adopts it as a finding. A source claim should not be stored as a panel finding.

## Important source-status distinctions

- Mira Lorne's memo is a staff claim, not a panel finding.
- Jun Park's letter is a contractor claim, not a panel finding.
- The panel finding is only that the west gate notice was missing before the April 13 closure.
- Claim SC-17 was disputed and not approved.
