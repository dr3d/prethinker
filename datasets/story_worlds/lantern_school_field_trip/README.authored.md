# Lantern School Field Trip

## Premise

Thirty-two students from Lantern Hill School go on a three-day coastal field trip. They split into four color-coded groups with assigned chaperones. Over three days, students swap groups, miss stops due to illness, rejoin at different points, chaperones substitute for each other, and two conflicting incident reports are filed about the same event at the tide pools.

## Intended Difficulty

Hard. The fixture attacks **group membership, absences, and "all/except" logic**. Questions require tracking who was in which group at which time, who was absent from which stop, and resolving conflicting accounts of the tide pool incident.

## Domain

School administration / field trip logistics / incident reporting
