## 13. District policy reference

Hamilton-Worthing School District Field Trip Policy **FT-2024.3 §6.4** specifies a chaperone-to-student ratio of **1:10 or better** for grades 7–8 field trips off school property. With 79 students and 12 chaperones (excluding the nurse), the trip ratio of approximately 1:6.58 is within policy.

District policy **FT-2024.3 §7.2** requires per-student check-in/check-out at "each major program transition." The policy does not explicitly enumerate "Schoolhouse Demonstration to bus reload" as a major program transition; this is one of the questions referred to the May 1 review.
