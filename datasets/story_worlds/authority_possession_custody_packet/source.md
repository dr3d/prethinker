## 0. Compiler's Note

This packet collects the governing documents, intake records, access logs, counsel guidance, and the open dispute notice concerning the Halberd Collection. It is intended to give trustees a single reference for the upcoming June 2026 arbitration. Items are reproduced in their original form as far as is practical; line breaks and headers may differ slightly from the source documents.

The Halberd Collection consists of forty-seven items donated by the Halberd Family Trust in 2022 and currently subject to a partial conservation engagement and a partial library-access arrangement.

---

## 1. Donor Agreement — 2022-09-14

**Parties.** The Halberd Family Trust ("the Trust"), as donor, and the Pellico Society ("the Society"), as donee.

**Subject.** The forty-seven items enumerated in Schedule A, collectively the "Halberd Collection," consisting of: one personal diary kept by Edmund Halberd between 1907 and 1912 ("Notebook A"); one business ledger kept by Edmund Halberd between 1915 and 1920 ("Notebook B"); the 1923 Halberd Mills bound ledger; twenty-four personal letters addressed to or written by Edmund Halberd; one bound photograph album; and nineteen loose photographs.

**Transfer.** The Trust hereby transfers legal title to the Halberd Collection, in its entirety, to the Society, effective on the date of this agreement.

**Reservation.** The Trust reserves a right of pre-publication review over publication by the Society of any item within the Collection that constitutes "personal correspondence" of Edmund Halberd. This right shall remain in force for five years from the effective date and may be extended by mutual written agreement. The Trust does not reserve a right to refuse research access, including reading-room access, to such items; the right reserved is solely a right of pre-publication review.

**Definition.** "Personal correspondence" is defined within this agreement to mean Notebook A and the twenty-four personal letters. The remaining items, including Notebook B, the 1923 Halberd Mills bound ledger, the photograph album, and the nineteen loose photographs, are not personal correspondence for the purposes of this clause.

**Signatures.** For the Trust, T. Halberd, Trustee. For the Society, J. Aronovich, Director.

## 2. Amendment to the Donor Agreement — 2024-02-05

The parties amend the Donor Agreement of 2022-09-14 as follows.

**Conservation custody.** The Society may, at its discretion, place items of the Halberd Collection into the temporary physical custody of an external conservation contractor for the purpose of conservation, restoration, or documentation work. Such temporary custody does not transfer legal title and does not transfer the conservation contractor any right of access, publication, or alienation.

**Recall.** The Society retains the right to recall any item from a contractor's custody on demand.

**Notice.** Consent of the Trust shall not be required for placement of items into contractor custody, but the Society shall notify the Trust within thirty days of any such placement of personal correspondence.

**Signatures.** For the Trust, T. Halberd, Trustee. For the Society, J. Aronovich, Director.

## 4. Memorandum of Understanding — Northbridge University Special Collections — 2023-05-20

**Parties.** Pellico Society and Northbridge University Special Collections ("Northbridge").

**Purpose.** To provide academic researchers with a hosted finding aid and a reading-room access point for digitized materials from the Halberd Collection.

**Scope.** This MOU applies only to items of the Halberd Collection that the Society has digitized and made available to Northbridge in image form. The MOU does not transfer legal title, physical custody of originals, or publication authority.

**Items included as of MOU effective date.** Notebook B (digitized 2023-04-12).

**Items added since MOU effective date.** None.

**Items expressly excluded.** Notebook A and the twenty-four personal letters are not within the scope of this MOU. The 1923 Halberd Mills bound ledger, the photograph album, and the nineteen loose photographs are also not within the scope of this MOU.

**Reading-room access.** Northbridge may operate a reading room for the digitized images of Notebook B. Northbridge does not have authority to grant access to the original Notebook B, which remains at the Society's premises.

**Publication.** Researchers wishing to publish images derived from the digitized Notebook B must obtain written permission from the Society. Northbridge is not authorized to grant such permission.

**Signed.** For Northbridge, P. Vu, Head of Special Collections. For the Society, J. Aronovich.

## 6. Counsel Memorandum — Latham & Carr LLP — 2026-03-18

To: J. Aronovich, Pellico Society.
From: A. Carr, Partner.
Re: Scope of pre-publication review reserved by the Halberd Family Trust.

**Question presented.** Does the right of pre-publication review reserved by the Trust under the 2022 Donor Agreement apply only to facsimile reproduction of personal correspondence, or does it also apply to transcripts and excerpts?

**Conclusion.** It is our opinion that, as the agreement is drafted, the reserved right applies to facsimile reproduction. The agreement uses the word "publication" in conjunction with the phrase "personal correspondence," and our reading is that publication of an underlying transcript or excerpt that does not reproduce the document image is not within the reserved right. We acknowledge that the Trust does not share this reading. The Trust's position, communicated by letter dated 2026-02-21, is that the reserved right covers any publication that conveys the textual content of the personal correspondence, including transcripts and excerpts.

**Recommendation.** Until the dispute is resolved, the Society should refrain from publication of any form (facsimile, transcript, or excerpt) of personal correspondence. This is a more conservative course than our legal reading would require, but it preserves the relationship and avoids litigation over a sympathetic counterparty.

**Counsel's caveats.** This memorandum offers our reading and does not bind the Trust or any tribunal. We further note that the agreement does not address the conservation documentation photographs that Stille produces in the ordinary course of its work; whether those photographs are "facsimile reproductions" within the reserved right is a question we do not resolve here.

## 7. Board Resolution — Pellico Society — 2026-04-02 — Resolution 2026-08

The Board of Trustees of the Pellico Society resolves as follows.

1. The Society shall not publish any personal correspondence of Edmund Halberd, in any form, until the dispute over the scope of the reserved right of pre-publication review has been resolved through binding arbitration.

2. Reading-room access to personal correspondence at the Society's premises and at the conservator's premises is not affected by this resolution. The reserved right concerns publication, not access.

3. The Society shall offer to participate in binding arbitration with the Trust to resolve the dispute. The arbitrator's determination shall be final.

4. The Society shall not, without further board action, treat the conservation documentation photographs produced by Stille as publishable images.

Voted unanimously, 2026-04-02.
