# Challenge Strategy — The Greenhouse Quarantine
## Primary Attack Surface
**Temporal status and scope expansion**: Lots move through suspect → confirmed → destroyed, or suspect → cleared, or precautionary hold → cleared, on different timelines. The quarantine scope expands twice (undisclosed movement, mistaken transfer). Questions require tracking each lot's status at specific dates.
## Secondary Surfaces
- **Subset splitting**: Lot 5C splits into 5 quarantined and 10 not-quarantined plants
- **Non-retraction updates**: Solberg doesn't retract "separate bench" but still elevates Lot 5B
- **Scope boundary precision**: Lot 5C was NOT quarantined initially because of arrival timing
- **Destruction deadline arithmetic**: 14 calendar days from order date
## Expected Failure Modes
- Treating all of Lot 5C as quarantined when only 5 of 15 plants were affected
- Treating Lot 5B as still suspect after it was cleared September 22
- Treating the "separate bench" note as retracted when Solberg explicitly did not retract it
- Quarantining Lot 5C from August 28 when it had no exposure overlap
