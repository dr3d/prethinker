# The Greenhouse Quarantine

## Premise
A plant health quarantine starts with one suspect lot and expands through disclosed and undisclosed movements, lab results, mistaken plant transfers, and reclassifications. Lots move through suspect, precautionary hold, confirmed positive, destroyed, cleared, and not-quarantined states. One lot is split into two subsets with different statuses.

## Intended Difficulty
Hard. Attacks **temporal status and scope expansion**. Questions require tracking which lot was in which status at which time, why each reclassification happened, and distinguishing between lots that were never quarantined, lots that were quarantined and cleared, and lots that were quarantined and destroyed.

## Domain
Agricultural regulation / plant health / quarantine management
