## Section D. Disputes and Pending Matters

### D.1 Codicil dispute

A handwritten note dated 2024-09-12, recovered from inside notebook
NB-2002-F (item EX-006) at the time of post-mortem inventory, purports
to bequeath "all my notebooks and digital archives" to Eliza
Yamamoto-Holloway. The note is signed "M. Holloway" and witnessed by
"V. Reeder."

Eliza Yamamoto-Holloway, through counsel L. Park, contends the note is a
valid holographic codicil under Berwick County probate practice and
asks that EX-004, EX-005, EX-006, EX-007, EX-008, and EX-011 be
distributed to her on that basis.

Daniel Holloway objects to the codicil on grounds of (i) authenticity,
(ii) testamentary capacity at the date of execution, and (iii)
ambiguity as to scope ("digital archives" not defined).

The Court ordered forensic handwriting analysis on 2026-03-19 (Order
P-26-347-C). The analysis report is not yet on file as of the compilation
date. An evidentiary hearing was originally calendared for 2026-04-22.

### D.2 Caryatid Study gift claim

Daniel Holloway asserts that EX-003 ("Caryatid Study", R. Bess, 1976)
was given to him by the decedent in person on 2024-11-19 and that the
museum's continued physical custody after that date was on his behalf,
not on the estate's. The estate's records show no transfer of title; the
museum's records list the Estate as lender. Mr. Holloway has filed a
motion for declaratory ruling (Motion 2026-03-04). No ruling has issued.

### D.3 Reeder-held items

V. Reeder holds EX-006, EX-007, and EX-008 at his residence. The basis
for his initial possession is that the decedent transferred these items
to him in late 2024 for "safekeeping" while she traveled. Reeder
asserts no claim of title and has stated, in correspondence dated
2026-01-12 to the executor, that he will "deliver these items to whom
the Court directs."

The executor has not yet directed delivery, taking the position that the
codicil dispute (D.1) must be resolved first. Reeder declines to
provide individual access in the interim. No party other than Reeder
currently has physical access to these three items, and Reeder has
indicated he will not personally inspect their contents pending Court
direction.

A motion to compel interim delivery of EX-006, EX-007, and EX-008 to
the executor was filed by Daniel Holloway on 2026-03-22 (Motion
P-26-347-M-3). The Court has not ruled on this motion.

## Section F. Recorded Statements and Their Standing

This register reproduces the assertions of parties as a matter of
record. Reproduction does not constitute a finding of fact. Specifically:

- The "private gift" assertion of Daniel Holloway regarding EX-003 is
  recorded but has not been ruled upon.
- The codicil contention of Eliza Yamamoto-Holloway is recorded but
  has not been ruled upon.

  found facts on this point.

The forensic handwriting analyst's report (when filed) and the Court's
ultimate rulings are the authoritative sources for findings on these
matters. This register is not.
