## 12. Pyxis override audit (system-generated)

Override classification rule **R-OV-2**: When override use case "patient medication" is selected for a high-alert anticoagulant on a unit with a two-RN witness requirement, the system shall require co-witness sign-on within 60 seconds of withdrawal.

For event 14:02:51, no co-witness sign-on occurred. The system flagged the event at 14:03:51. The flag was queued to pharmacy review.
