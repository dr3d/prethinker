# Bramwell Commercial Properties — Lease Amendment Register

## Purpose

This register tracks amendments, corrections, and supersessions to commercial leases managed by Bramwell Commercial Properties Ltd. It covers the period January 2024 through March 2025. Each entry records the original lease term, the amendment, who authorized it, and the current effective state.

## Lease Register

### Lease L-2019-004: Kettner & Associates (Suite 210)

**Original terms (signed March 2019):** 5-year lease, $4,200/month, expiry March 2024. Tenant responsible for interior maintenance. Landlord responsible for HVAC and structural.

**Amendment A1 (October 2023):** Lease extended 2 years to March 2026. Rent increased to $4,500/month effective April 2024. Authorized by Property Manager Helen Corr.

**Amendment A2 (June 2024):** Rent further increased to $4,800/month effective July 2024, reflecting market adjustment clause in A1. Authorized by Corr.

**Correction C1 (August 2024):** Amendment A2's effective date corrected from July 2024 to August 2024. Reason: the market adjustment clause requires 60 days' notice. A2 was signed June 15 with a July 1 effective date, but 60 days from June 15 is August 14. Corr issued a correction notice: "The rent increase to $4,800 takes effect August 14, 2024, not July 1, 2024. Kettner is owed a refund of $600 for the July 1–August 13 overcharge (44 days at $20/day differential)."

**Current state:** $4,800/month effective August 14, 2024. Lease expires March 2026. Refund of $600 owed to Kettner.

### Lease L-2020-011: Primrose Day Nursery (Suite 105)

**Original terms (signed September 2020):** 3-year lease, $3,100/month, expiry September 2023. Tenant responsible for all interior including HVAC filter changes. Landlord responsible for structural and roof.

**Amendment A1 (August 2023):** Lease extended 3 years to September 2026. Rent unchanged at $3,100/month for the first extension year, then $3,400/month from September 2024. Authorized by Corr.

**Amendment A2 (January 2024):** Added a shared outdoor play area (Courtyard B) to the lease at no additional rent, subject to the tenant maintaining the space. Authorized by Senior Property Manager David Pryce, who overruled Corr's recommendation against the amendment. Pryce's note: "The nursery is a valued long-term tenant. The courtyard addition strengthens retention."

**Supersession S1 (November 2024):** Amendment A2 superseded. Courtyard B removed from the lease effective December 1, 2024. Reason: Bramwell received complaints from adjacent tenants about noise during nursery outdoor time. Authorized by Pryce. Pryce's note: "The courtyard was added in good faith but has created an untenable situation for adjacent tenants. The nursery may use Courtyard C as a substitute, subject to a separate scheduling agreement."

**Voided amendment V1 (February 2025):** The nursery's lease file contained an unsigned draft amendment dated October 2024 that would have reduced rent to $2,900/month. Corr's note: "This draft was prepared during rent renegotiation discussions but was never signed by either party. It has no legal effect. Remove from active file. Retain in archive for record-keeping."

**Current state:** $3,400/month effective September 2024. Courtyard B removed (superseded December 2024). Courtyard C available under separate scheduling agreement (not part of the lease). Lease expires September 2026. Draft rent reduction ($2,900) has no effect.

### Lease L-2021-007: Strand Electronics (Suite 340)

**Original terms (signed April 2021):** 4-year lease, $5,600/month, expiry April 2025. Includes reserved parking (4 spaces, Lot A).

**Amendment A1 (March 2024):** Lease extended 3 years to April 2028. Rent increased to $5,900/month effective May 2024. Parking increased to 6 spaces (4 in Lot A, 2 in Lot B). Authorized by Corr.

**Correction C1 (September 2024):** Parking assignment corrected. Amendment A1 stated "2 in Lot B" but Lot B was reassigned to another property in July 2024. Correction assigns the 2 additional spaces to Lot C instead. Authorized by Pryce. Pryce's note: "This is a landlord error. Strand is not at fault and should not bear any parking disruption."

**Amendment A2 (January 2025):** Strand requested a right of first refusal on Suite 342 (adjacent). Corr approved. No rent change. The right of first refusal expires with the lease (April 2028) and does not obligate Bramwell to offer Suite 342 — it only requires that Bramwell notify Strand before leasing 342 to another party.

**Retraction R1 (March 2025):** Strand withdrew its right of first refusal on Suite 342. Strand's letter: "We no longer anticipate needing additional space. Please remove the right of first refusal from our lease record." Corr acknowledged and removed the right effective March 15, 2025.

**Current state:** $5,900/month effective May 2024. 6 parking spaces (4 Lot A, 2 Lot C — corrected from Lot B). Right of first refusal on Suite 342 retracted March 15, 2025. Lease expires April 2028.

### Lease L-2022-015: Vacant (Suite 420)

**Original terms (signed January 2022):** 3-year lease to Holbrook Design, $4,000/month, expiry January 2025. Standard maintenance terms.

**Early termination (October 2024):** Holbrook Design exercised an early termination clause, effective November 30, 2024. Termination fee of $8,000 paid. Corr verified the clause and accepted the termination.

**Post-vacancy:** Suite 420 has been vacant since December 1, 2024. A prospective tenant, Navarro Consulting, submitted a letter of intent on February 10, 2025 for a 5-year lease at $4,400/month. Corr reviewed and recommended acceptance to Pryce.

**Current state:** Vacant. No active lease. Holbrook's lease terminated November 30, 2024. Navarro's letter of intent is under review — it is not a binding lease.

### Authorization Hierarchy Note

Corr (Property Manager) may authorize standard amendments, corrections, and operational changes valued under $50,000 annually. Pryce (Senior Property Manager) must authorize amendments involving common-area changes, tenant disputes, corrections of landlord errors, or actions overruling a Property Manager recommendation. Any amendment valued over $50,000 requires Board approval. No Board-level amendments appear in this register.
