## Section 4. Adult Roles and Chaperone Ratio

Cedar Hollow Public Schools policy CHPS-OF-12 (Off-Site Activity
Standards) requires a minimum chaperone ratio of **1 chaperone per 10
students** for grades 7–8 off-site overnight activities. Policy
SCO-CH-3 (Chaperone Counting Rules) defines who counts toward the
ratio.

Per SCO-CH-3, the following adult roles are recognized:

- **Chaperone:** an adult assigned to general supervision, with
  authority to direct student behavior and conduct headcount.
  **Counts toward ratio.**
- **Medical staff:** an adult assigned exclusively to medical coverage.
  Cannot be diverted to general supervision. **Does not count toward
  ratio.**
- **Driver:** the licensed bus driver. While in transit, may not leave
  the driver's seat. **Does not count toward ratio.**
- **Parent observer:** a parent or guardian permitted by the
  Activities Director to observe specific events. Has no supervisory
  authority. **Does not count toward ratio.**
