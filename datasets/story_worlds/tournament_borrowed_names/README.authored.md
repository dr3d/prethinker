# The Tournament of Borrowed Names

## Premise
An archery tournament where competitors use inherited banner names. Two competitors gave up their old banners and created new ones, meaning the same title ("the Owl") refers to different people in different years. One eliminated competitor becomes a range officer; another becomes a scorer. Questions must track which person is behind which banner at which time.

## Intended Difficulty
Hard. Attacks **aliasing and identity drift**. "The Owl won in 2023" and "the Owl scored 281" refer to different people. A scoring error swaps two ranks. An eliminated competitor who becomes a range officer makes a judgment call that affects the match outcome.

## Domain
Sports administration / tournament records / identity tracking
