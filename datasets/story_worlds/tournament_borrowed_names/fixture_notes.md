# Challenge Strategy — The Tournament of Borrowed Names
## Primary Attack Surface
**Aliasing and identity drift**: "The Owl" means Kester Mace in 2023 and Orla Venn in 2025. "The Stag" means Bryce Emmet in 2022 and Nessa Calloway in 2025. Questions require resolving which person is behind which banner at which time, and not attributing one person's achievements to another.
## Secondary Surfaces
- **Role changes**: An eliminated competitor becomes a range officer and makes a ruling that affects a match. Another eliminated competitor becomes a scorer and certifies the final result.
- **Scoring correction**: The original qualifying ranks had a transcription error. The corrected ranks change who advances.
- **Dual records**: Emmet is both a registered competitor and a range officer. Venn is both a registered competitor and a scorer.
- **Championship attribution**: The Stag's 2022 championship belongs to Emmet, not Calloway, even though Calloway now holds the banner.
## Expected Failure Modes
- Treating the 2023 Owl championship as Orla Venn's when it was Kester Mace's
- Treating the 2022 Stag championship as Calloway's when it was Emmet's
- Treating the original qualifying ranks as correct when they contain a tabulation error
- Confusing Emmet's role as range officer with his role as competitor
