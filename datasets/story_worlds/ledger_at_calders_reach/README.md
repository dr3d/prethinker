# The Ledger at Calder's Reach

This fixture is a dense long-story frontier project for Prethinker.

It pressures identity tracking, aliases, changing ownership, temporary and
permanent roles, guardianship, residence rules, inheritance, district-based
eligibility, boundary law, and final-state reasoning across several years.

## Files

- `story.md`: source story to compile.
- `source.md`: preserved copy of the story under the generic source name.
- `qa.md`: script-compatible numbered QA file extracted from the supplied QA
  source.
- `progress_journal.md`: running mini-journal of benchmark attempts and lessons.
- `progress_metrics.jsonl`: append-only metrics rows for progress graphs.

## Evidence-Lane Discipline

The supplied `qa_source.md` also contained canonical entity inventories,
suggested ontology/predicate families, state snapshots, gold triples, and
scoring advice. Those materials are oracle/strategy content and are **not**
admitted into this fixture.

This fixture intentionally has no `gold_kb.pl`, no `ontology_registry.json`, no
`intake_plan.md`, no `qa_source.md`, and no `failure_buckets.json` at admission
time. Cold runs should use only `story.md` as the source document. `qa.md`
exists for post-ingestion scoring, not source-compilation context.

No benchmark run has been recorded yet.

## Why It Matters

The fixture pressures:

- long-range identity and alias resolution across years;
- state-at-time ownership and role queries;
- legal instrument triggers and automatic transfers;
- temporary authority versus permanent ownership or office;
- possession versus legal title;
- guardianship start/end conditions;
- scholarship eligibility with district-residence limits;
- avulsion versus gradual boundary drift;
- final-state reasoning after multiple corrections and discoveries.
