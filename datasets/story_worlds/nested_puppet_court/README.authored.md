# Nested Puppet Court

## Premise

During a village hearing to resolve a land boundary dispute between two families, a traveling puppet troupe performs a cautionary play about greed and false testimony. The play-within-the-hearing contains accusations, confessions, and judgments that mirror — but do not match — the real dispute. Witnesses reference the puppet play in their testimony, sometimes quoting puppet dialogue as if it were real precedent.

## Intended Difficulty

Hard. The fixture attacks **quoted-world contamination**: the tendency for AI systems to treat statements made within a fictional frame (the puppet play) as facts about the real hearing. Questions require distinguishing between real hearing testimony, puppet dialogue, witness references to puppet dialogue, and the actual findings of the hearing officer.

## Domain

Village governance / land dispute / nested fictional frame
