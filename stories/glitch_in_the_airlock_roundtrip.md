# The Glitch in the Airlock (Roundtrip Story)

## Story Text

Jax was a freelance space-salvager with neon-blue hair and a bad habit of borrowing oxygen without asking.
While scouting the Nebula Sector, her thrusters flickered out, forcing her to dock at a sleek chrome-plated dome floating on an asteroid.
Jax asked whether the station was occupied, but only silence answered.

### The Fuel Cells

In the galley, three canisters of liquid star-fuel were plugged into a charging station.
Jax was running on empty, so she took a sip.
She tapped into the mega-cell and found it too radioactive.
Her teeth glowed green after the mega-cell.
She tried the eco-cell and found it too sluggish.
She felt like she had swallowed molasses after the eco-cell.
She tried the nano-cell, and it was just right.
Her jetpack hummed with perfect crystalline energy.

### The Gravity Boots

In the mudroom, three pairs of anti-gravity boots were docked.
Jax stepped into the titan boots, but they were too heavy.
She could not lift her feet off the floor.
She tried the hover-slippers, but they were too floaty.
She bounced off the ceiling and hit her head.
She then wore the sonic-zips, and they were just right.
She zoomed around the room until she pushed them too hard.
The sonic-zips blew a fuse and became a charred mess.

### The Sleep Pods

Exhausted, Jax found the sleeping quarters.
She tried the cryo-chamber, but it was too cold.
She tried the memory-foam cloud, but it was too squishy.
She climbed into the bio-hammock, and it was just right.
Jax fell into a deep sleep in the bio-hammock.

### The Return of the Circuit-Family

The airlock cycled open and Unit-Alpha, Unit-Beta, and Widget returned from a morning spacewalk.
Unit-Alpha reported that someone had siphoned his high-grade isotopes.
Unit-Beta reported that someone had tasted her organic fuel.
Widget reported that someone had drunk his nano-cell and emptied the vial.

They moved into the mudroom.
Unit-Alpha reported that someone had tried to calibrate his titan boots.
Unit-Beta reported scuff marks on her hover-slippers.
Widget reported that someone wore his sonic-zips and fried the motherboards.

They moved into the sleeping quarters.
Unit-Alpha reported that a biological entity had been in his cryo-chamber.
Unit-Beta reported that her memory-foam cloud was still compressed.
Widget saw a human asleep in his bio-hammock.

Jax woke up, saw the three bots, activated her jetpack, and did not wait for an explanation.
She performed a perfect zero-gravity backflip through the airlock and vanished into the starfield.
Widget watched her go and hoped she would leave a review on the Galactic Map.
Widget said she really liked his fuel.
